About Us


FinCludeSecure International is an advisory engagement practise with extensive experience and competence in FinTech, AgriTech, Digital Payment & Financial Services and Telecommunication. We are focused on advisory, research, product development and service delivery in Digital Payments & Financial Services with bias in financial inclusion for the undeserved/poor, security and gender equality. With our bias, we strive to contribute to the social economics of the countries in which we operate as a supporter of sustainable growth and national development.

At FinCludeSecure International , We lead and engage leveraging on our globally experienced resource; delivering value to our clients; through value prepositions of sustainable development and growth, while supporting with capacity building in the different change management programs for organizations and governments. We bring a strong multi-disciplinary teams to bear that is supported by proven consulting, analytical and delivery methodologies with capacity building approach to local content.

Our Vision


To improve the living conditions and standards for humanity; a human being at a time, by promoting financial inclusion and proper gender representation in capacity and nation building.
